Mars & Saturn

Mars

Eyes: Green | Age: 1 Year | Weight: 11 lbs
Breed: Domestic Short Hair (Orange Tabby)
Nicknames: Marsie-Mars, The (Big) Orange Menace, Baby Boy, My Little Sweet Potato
Hobbies: finding warm places to hide, chasing Saturn, catching (and eating) flies, getting brushed, and talking back
Adoption Story:
I was walking home from work and this couple asked me if I liked cats. The guy had a whole litter of kittens in his sweatshirt pocket, begging for people to give these kittens a good home because they couldn't take care of them. He handed me Mars and I couldn't say no to the little cutie.

Saturn

Eyes: Green | Age: 7 Months | Weight: 6 lbs
Breed: Domestic Short Hair (Black Tabby)
Nicknames: Little Princess, Baby Girl, Garbage Disposal (very food-motivated), Little Complainer
Hobbies: Demanding attention/pets, cuddling, splootin', catching (and eating) flies, and head-tilting when confused
Adoption Story:
Mars had a serious case of Single Kitten Syndrome so my boyfriend and I decided we wanted to get him a friend. We went to our local shelter, looked around at several different cats until we saw Saturn (known as Tipper at the shelter) and she started purring like crazy. I knew that we had found our new kitten.
